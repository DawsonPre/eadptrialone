Author

Dawson Preethi has been a structural engineer for the past 24 years.
He has spent the last two decades studying quantum mechanics, philosophy, literature, and psychoanalysis as a self-awareness attempt and background activity in all the spare time he has discovered when he is not engaged in academic studies.
During his tenor, he worked for eleven different companies in Egypt, Cameroon, the United Arab Emirates, the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and the Sultanate of Oman. He holds a BSc in engineering from the University of Moratuwa in Sri Lanka, as well as an MSc in structural and foundation engineering from Heriot-Watt University in the United Kingdom.
He is a Project Management Professional PMP, and he and his wife live in Sri Lanka..
